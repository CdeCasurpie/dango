What is Dango World?

Dango World is an ultra-smooth, physics-based multiplayer sandbox. Connect with friends and bounce around the cosmos using our custom soft-body physics engine! Every collision, jump, and squish is calculated in real-time.

Whether you are playing on your browser using HTML5 Canvas or directly on your desktop via the natively compiled Python engine, you'll experience pixel-perfect synchronization and the exact same adorable aesthetics. There is no central game server restricting your movement; host it yourself with a single script and invite your friends.

Explore unique planets, discover hidden easter eggs, and customize your Dango with an array of colors and hats (coming soon!).

Main Features

Cross-Play

Seamlessly connect Desktop and Web players with ultra-low latency WebSockets. Play anywhere.

Open Source

Built with Python and aiohttp. Host your own server instantly and mod the physics engine.

Live Chat

Communicate in real-time with other dangos. Your messages appear right above your soft head!
